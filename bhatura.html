Bhatura Recipe | Punjabi Bhature Recipe | Bhatura for Chole Bhatura


I would like to share some tips and tricks for perfect, fluffy bahtura recipe. to get a perfect shape, kneading the dough is very critical. i have kneaded the dough for almost 8-10 minutes continuously. also, i have added 1 tbsp of rava while mixing the dough. rava helps to get a crispy bhature after deep frying. lastly, i would highly recommend to use sour curd or yogurt. sourness would hasten the fermentation process and the dough would be extra soft.

PREP TIME : 2 hr 30 minutes     COOK TIME : 20 minutes     TOTAL TIME : 2 hr 30 minutes





Ingredients

  • 2 cups maida / all-purpose flour / plain flour
  • 1 tbsp rava / semolina
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • salt to taste
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp baking soda / cooking soda
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 1 cup curd / yogurt



Instructions

BHATURA DOUGH RECIPE:

  1. firstly, in a large mixing bowl take maida.
  2. to that add rava, sugar and salt.
  3. also add baking powder and baking soda and mix well.
  4. furthermore, add oil and rub with the flour.
  6. also add curd and start to knead dough.
  8. knead and punch the dough well for atleast 5 minutes.
  9. further, grease the dough with oil.
  10. cover with moist cloth and rest for atleast 2 hours.
  11. further, roll and get to a long shape. pinch the dough into medium sized balls.
  12. then make balls between your palms.
  13. finally, roll the dough evenly into circles using rolling pin. roll neither too thin nor thick.

BHATURA DOUGH RECIPE:

  1. firstly, heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadai. when the oil is sufficiently hot, add one bhatura.
  2. and, press with the spoon to puff up.
  3. also once they begin to puff, splash oil over the bhatura till they puff completely.
  4. flip over and fry the bhatura till golden brown all over.
  5. then drain the poori into tissue paper to remove excess oil. fry all batura same way.
  6. finally, serve bhatura with chole curry.
